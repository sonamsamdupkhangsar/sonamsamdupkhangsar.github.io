# Sign up using email
The first step is setting up a user account. 
From home `https://authzmanager.sonam.cloud` you can browse to `sign-up page` by clicking on Signup link. ![authzmanager.sonam.cloud](images/home.png) 

On the Signup page enter your first and lastname with email and a username and password:

![https://authzmanager.sonam.cloud/signup](images/signup.png)



Then you can hit signup button to create an account.  ![signup success](images/signup-success.png)

The signup event will send a email to activate your account.  Click on the link to activate your account. 
![activate account](images/activate-account.png). 

When you click on the link it will open a url to activate your account.  Now you can go to the Auth Manager app to create your OAuth2 aplication by click on Auth manager link.  
![account activated](images/account-activated.png)



To log into the Auth Manager click on `Login`.
![home link](images/home.png)

Enter your username and password to login.
![login](images/login.png).

On success login you will see the following Admin page. ![login success](images/login-success.png)


In [next](../create-oauth2-app/README.md) I will create OAuth2 Client, organization and roles.
